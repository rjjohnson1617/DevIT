What is DevIT Connect?

DevIT Connect is a community of people that are passionate about anything computer related! We want to create connections, learn from one another, and help one another. Any skill level and age is welcome. We just want to learn about computer science in a fun, informal environment.

When and Where Do We Meet?

Since we are only in the beginning, we do not have a set date, time, or location for these meetups. As the community grows, we will become more organized and hopefully move to a monthly, or more frequent, format. As for now, we will post dates, times, and locations on the calendar or on our Facebook page.
